Attending Digital Migration practicals was an exciting and enlightening experience.

It felt empowering to translate theoretical knowledge into real-world applications, working with advanced satellite equipment and understanding the intricacies of signal alignment.

The hands-on nature of the training boosted confidence, as each successful installation brought a sense of achievement.

Engaging with mentors and peers added a collaborative dimension, making the learning process both productive and enjoyable.

While there were challenges, such as fine-tuning the dish alignment or troubleshooting unexpected signal issues, overcoming them was deeply satisfying.

Overall, the practicals left one feeling more skilled, confident, and prepared to handle professional installations.

Technical support/ hardware

Participating in the Technical Support practical was a game-changer for me. From diagnosing hardware issues to resolving software glitches, every task pushed me to think critically and act confidently. The hands-on experience with troubleshooting tools and real-life scenarios made theory come alive. It was rewarding to see problems solved through persistence and teamwork. I walked away feeling more skilled, prepared, and excited for the challenges of the tech support field.

cell-phone Repair

As a woman in the cell phone repair field, the practical experience was both empowering and inspiring. Learning to disassemble and reassemble devices, troubleshoot hardware issues, and replace components boosted my confidence in handling tools and technology. The support from instructors and peers created an encouraging environment, proving that this field has room for everyone. By the end of the training, I felt equipped to tackle repairs independently and proud to break barriers in a male-dominated industry.
