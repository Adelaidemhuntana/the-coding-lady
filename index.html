About The Coding Lady Cooperative

Leading Change Through Technology

The Coding Lady cooperative is a dynamic, women-led initiative that focuses on providing cutting-edge solutions in web development, WiFi installation, solar cleaning and installation, and cell phone repair.

We believe in harnessing the power of technology to empower communities and businesses. Our team is committed to delivering high-quality, reliable services tailored to meet your unique needs.

By choosing us, you’re supporting not just innovation but also the empowerment of women in technology. Together, we strive to break barriers and make a lasting impact in the tech industry.

We are proud to offer innovative solutions that can transform your business, enhance your connectivity, and help you adopt sustainable energy solutions.

Join us in making a difference and let us help you grow with technology.

  • Expert Web Development Solutions
  • Reliable WiFi Installation Services
  • Eco-friendly Solar Installation & Maintenance
  • Fast and Efficient Cell Phone Repair
